GOtrack
 
 

Description

GOtrack is a user-friendly tool to browse the development of gene product annotations

GOtrack currently supports annotation data for 14 different organisms

Explore

  • Changes in the Gene Ontology (GO) terms associated to a gene product

  • Changes in the gene products associated to a GO term

  • Measures of semantic similarity and multifunctionality between years
